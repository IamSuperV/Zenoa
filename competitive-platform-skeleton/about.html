Mission

We are quantifying the world's decision-making through competitive scenarios.

The Problem

Standard tests measure what you know, not how you think. In the real world, "correct" answers rarely exist.

The Solution

Zenoa turns situational judgment into a measurable science. real-time simulations where your trade-offs, speed, and consistency build a high-fidelity map of your cognitive profile.
